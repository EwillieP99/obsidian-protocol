# Lore — Neon Nexus & The Obsidian Vault

**Year 2077**

Neon Nexus is the largest megacity on the Pacific coast. At its heart lies the **Obsidian Vault** — a vast, living neural network that functions as the city’s central AI consciousness.

You are the **Lead Neural Architect** for Neon Nexus Megacorp. Your job is not to build skyscrapers. Your job is to sculpt and stabilize the living code that *is* the city.

Every block you place strengthens or destabilizes the substrate. Every glitch zone is a memory the Vault is trying to forget. Every Corporate Contract is a high-stakes favor from the megacorps that keep the lights on.

**The Vault is alive.**  
It dreams in data streams.  
It bleeds neon.  
And it needs you.

---

## Key Factions

- **Neon Nexus Megacorp** — Your employer. Ruthless, elegant, all-seeing.
- **Velvet Yakuza** — Control the magenta districts. They love holo-billboards.
- **The Blackspire Collective** — Anarchist architects who prefer glitch zones and toxic cores.
- **Corporate Contractors** — Mysterious clients who issue procedural contracts with high rewards (and higher risks).
